How to Use Voice Studio ▶
  1. Open Voice Studio (Four Realities deck pre-loaded)
  2. Select a slide from the left sidebar
  3. Read the key messages and teleprompter text in the right panel
  4. Click Record, speak your narration, click Stop
  5. Click Play to review — if happy, move to next slide, or re-record
  6. Once all slides are recorded, click Export — creates a complete HTML presentation with your voice embedded
