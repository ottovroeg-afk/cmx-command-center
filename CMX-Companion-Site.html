The Framework

Three Types of Digital Companion

Each tool we build falls into one of three value layers. This is how we talk about what AI does for AKT — and for our clients.

Layer 1

Efficiency

Save time on things we already do. Replace manual, repetitive work with instant automation. Same output, fraction of the effort.

"Instead of spending 3 days building a workshop deck, it takes 10 minutes."

Layer 2

Scale

Let a small team handle more clients, more complexity, more markets — without hiring proportionally. Multiply what each consultant can deliver.

"One consultant manages 12 stakeholders across 14 regions with real-time intelligence."

Layer 3

Innovation

Enable entirely new capabilities. AI agents, MCP-based architecture, predictive models — things that weren't possible before, now delivered as part of our advisory.

"An agent connects to the client's data lake and interacts with their systems directly."
